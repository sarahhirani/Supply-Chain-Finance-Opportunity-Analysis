# Supply-Chain-Finance-Opportunity-Analysis
**Supply Chain Finance Opportunity Analysis**

This project analyzes the potential impact of implementing a **Supply Chain Finance (SCF) program** to improve supplier liquidity and optimize working capital across a supplier network.

The analysis evaluates:

• Supplier financing cost savings
• Working capital improvements
• Program adoption likelihood
• Supplier segmentation by industry and region
• Potential financing volume and bank revenue

**Project Objectives**

The goal of this project is to demonstrate how financial institutions and corporate treasury teams evaluate the feasibility of Supply Chain Finance programs.

The analysis focuses on identifying:

• Suppliers that would benefit most from early payment financing
• Regions and industries with the highest program opportunity
• Estimated financing volume and revenue potential for the financing institution

**Key Findings**

• Supply Chain Finance could finance approximately **$43.9M in supplier invoices**
• Estimated **$2.9M annual revenue opportunity** based on financing margin
• Suppliers experience an average **51-day improvement in liquidity**
• Smaller suppliers face higher **borrowing costs**, increasing program adoption likelihood

**Tools Used**

• **Excel** – financial modeling and KPI analysis
• **Python** – data preparation and synthetic dataset generation
• **Tableau** – interactive analytics dashboard

**Dashboard Visualizations**

• The Tableau dashboard includes:
• Supplier Savings by Industry
• Regional SCF Opportunity
• Top Opportunity Suppliers
• Borrowing Rate vs Adoption Analysis

These visuals illustrate how supplier borrowing costs, invoice volumes, and regional factors influence SCF adoption potential.
